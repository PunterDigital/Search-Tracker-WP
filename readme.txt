Search Tracker lets you see what users are searching for on your site.

== Description ==

Find what people are searching for on your site using Search Tracker.

Features:
* See what users are searching for on your site
* See what search is the most popular on your site
* See what search is the least popular on your site

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What does this plugin do? =

Search Tracker lets you see what your users are searching on your site. Allowing you to make more data-driven decisions to your content creation.
